Put You in Chicago and Put an Ice Cream in Your Mouth

What's your favorite flavor? Dec. 2020

Let's fast-forward to Summer 2021 when the new-normal imposed by COVID-19 doesn't feel so bad thanks to all the sweet, cold summer treats that Chicago has to offer. Visitors and locals alike can enjoy myriad ice cream venues, from the creative flavor combinations of Jeni's Splendid Ice Creams to classic sundaes and concretes at Lickity Split. In a hurry and can't venture off the beaten path? No worries, for Baskin-Robbins and other national chains abound. Mark you calendars for July 18th - National Ice Cream Day! See you in Chicago.

Data used in this project

  • Ice cream shop locations were provided by data from Open Street Map, and the ice cream svg is from FreeSVG.com.
  • The Chicago neighborhoods outlines and labels is from the City of Chicago Data Portal.
  • Additional layers come from MapBox default tilesets.

Tools used in this project

  • Data from Open Street Map was analyzed and geojsons created using QGIS 3.14
  • Data is shown on Mapbox web map.
